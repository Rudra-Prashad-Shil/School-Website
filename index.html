WELCOME

This school established as Jubilee Junior High School in Subarnachar Upazila of Noakhali district was named after the present Shaheed Zainal Abedin High School in 1970 in Harich Chowdhury Bazar of West Charjubili Village in Subarnachar Shaheed Jay Quarter of Independence.

DAILY ASSEMBLY

In a school, daily assembly refers to a short gathering of all the students in a designated area, usually in the morning before classes begin. It serves as a way to bring the entire school community together, promote unity, and communicate important information to students.

TREE PLANTATION

Tree plantation is the process of planting and growing trees in a particular area, often with the aim of increasing the number and density of trees in that location. It is an essential activity for maintaining a sustainable environment and combating various environmental issues.

HAPPY TEACHERS

It refers to a state where teachers experience satisfaction, motivation, and a sense of well-being in their profession with supportive school leadership, effective communication, work-life balance, professional development opportunities, recognition, and respect.
